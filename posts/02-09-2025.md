**THIS WEEK IN CRYPTO**
\- Bitcoin gained 3% year-to-date compared to gold's 9% rise as investors shift toward risk-off assets amid Trump's trade war threats and tariff policies. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-02-08/bitcoin-btc-lags-gold-as-trump-s-trade-war-spurs-safe-haven-demand).

\- Representatives William Timmons (R-SC) and Ritchie Torres (D-NY) released draft NFT legislation designating certain NFTs (artwork, gaming assets, memberships) as non-securities, aiming for inclusion in revised FIT21 digital asset framework. [Link](https://decrypt.co/304760/congressmen-nft-protections-revised-crypto-bill). [Draft bill](https://timmons.house.gov/uploadedfiles/12.20.24_nft_bill_text.pdf).

\- Two bipartisan stablecoin proposals mirror EU's MiCA requirements, potentially delisting Tether's USDT from U.S. exchanges. Bills mandate audits by major U.S. accounting firms—which Tether indicated it won't obtain. [Link](https://unchainedcrypto.com/stablecoin-bills-could-squeeze-out-140-billion-tether/).

\- Alexey Pertsev was released under electronic monitoring pending appeal after being sentenced to 64 months on money laundering charges in the Netherlands for his work on Tornado Cash. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/339401/tornado-cash-developer-alexey-pertsev-gets-nod-for-conditional-release-from-prison-to-work-on-appeal).

\- Ethereum raised gas limits for the first time in three years from 15 million to 32 million per block, expected to reduce transaction fees. [Link](https://decrypt.co/304318/ethereum-hikes-gas-limit-amid-historic-volatility).

\- Polymarket reached record 450,000 monthly active traders in January, though volumes declined from November's $2.6B peak to $1.23B. [Link](https://financefeeds.com/polymarket-hits-record-450k-traders-despite-election-cycle-ending/).

\- MicroStrategy rebranded as "Strategy" with a new Bitcoin-focused logo. [Link](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/microstrategy-rebrands-strategy-unveils-logo-213040129.html).

\- Senator Elizabeth Warren (D-MA) joined Republicans criticizing bank debanking of crypto firms—a notable departure from her previous anti-crypto stance. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/339053/sen-warren-crypto-debanking-criticizes-banks-closing-accounts-washington).

\- Jorge Tenreiro, the SEC's top crypto litigator, was reassigned to IT as the agency shifts away from "regulation by enforcement." [Link](https://www.wsj.com/finance/currencies/sec-ousts-top-litigator-who-battled-with-crypto-giants-558548a8).

\- Republican lawmakers and White House crypto czar David Sacks formed a working group targeting stablecoin legislation within Trump's first 100 days. [Link](https://www.axios.com/2025/02/04/trump-crypto-legislation-regulations).

\- MELANIA and TRUMP memecoins plummeted 90% and 75% respectively, losing over $12 billion collectively since post-inauguration launch. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-02-06/melania-memecoin-is-down-90-from-peak-as-novelty-value-wanes).

\- Franklin Templeton filed amended S-1 registration for a crypto index ETF initially tracking Bitcoin and Ethereum, with potential for additional assets pending SEC approval. [Link](https://decrypt.co/304919/franklin-templeton-sec-approval-crypto-index-etf).

\- Ondo Finance, the second-largest tokenized Treasuries issuer, announced plans for a dedicated layer-1 blockchain for real-world asset tokenization. [Link](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2025/02/06/ondo-finance-unveils-layer-1-network-for-tokenized-assets).

\- The Solana-based AI agent DAO ai16z rebranded to ElizaOS following venture firm a16z's request. [Link](https://unchainedcrypto.com/ai16z-is-rebranding-as-elizaos-after-request-from-venture-firm-a16z/).

\- The Winklevoss twins' exchange Gemini is exploring an initial public offering this year. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-02-06/winklevoss-twins-crypto-exchange-gemini-is-said-to-weigh-ipo).
