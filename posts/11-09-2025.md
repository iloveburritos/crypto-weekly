**THIS WEEK IN CRYPTO**
\- Bitcoin fell below $100,000 and altcoins experienced significant declines, as broader risk-on assets sold off on hawkish Fed comments indicating October's rate cut could be the last for the year. [Link](https://fortune.com/crypto/2025/11/06/the-crypto-market-may-be-out-of-gas-as-bitcoin-dips-under-100k-and-alt-coins-plummet/).

\- The crypto market declined 20% from its October 6 peak of $4.4 trillion, wiping out nearly all of 2025's gains and leaving the asset class up just 2.5% for the year. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-11-07/crypto-bear-market-wipes-out-almost-entire-2025-value-increase).

\- Stablecoin transaction volume on Ethereum reached a record $2.82 trillion in October. [Link](https://unchainedcrypto.com/stablecoin-volume-on-ethereum-breaks-2-8-trillion-record-in-october/).

\- The jury failed to reach a unanimous verdict in the fraud trial of two MIT graduate brothers accused of stealing $25 million in cryptocurrency in a sandwich attack, where bots are used to place large trades before and after another user's transactions. The judge declared a mistrial. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-11-08/judge-declares-mistrial-in-mit-grad-brothers-fraud-case). [Link](https://www.reuters.com/legal/government/mistrial-declared-mit-educated-brothers-accused-25-million-cryptocurrency-heist-2025-11-08/)

\- 55% of hedge funds hold crypto-related assets, up from 47% last year, with funds allocating an average of 7% of AUM to the asset class. [Link](https://www.reuters.com/business/more-than-half-hedge-funds-invested-crypto-global-survey-says-2025-11-06/).

\- Acting CFTC chair Caroline Pham confirmed plans to launch leveraged spot crypto trading on regulated exchanges, marking a significant regulatory shift toward crypto markets. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/378112/acting-cftc-chair-confirms-push-to-launch-leveraged-spot-crypto-trading-on-regulated-exchanges).

\- Kazakhstan plans to launch a $1 billion crypto reserve fund using seized assets by 2026. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377998/kazakhstan-to-launch-1-billion-crypto-reserve-fund-using-seized-assets-by-2026-bloomberg).

\- Block reported nearly $2 billion in bitcoin revenue in Q3, accounting for nearly one-third of its total revenues. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377971/block-q3-earnings).

\- The Ethereum validator queue grew to 1.5 million ETH waiting to start staking. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377317/ethereum-validator-queue-grows-with-1-5-million-eth-waiting-to-start-staking).

\- A Columbia University study found 25% of Polymarket's trading volume over the past three years was artificially inflated by wash trading, where users rapidly buy and sell the same contracts, [Link](https://fortune.com/2025/11/07/polymarket-wash-trading-inflated-prediction-markets-columbia-research/).

\- Japan's three largest banks (MUFG, Sumitomo Mitsui, and Mizuho) received regulatory approval for a yen stablecoin proof-of-concept trial, with plans to launch by March 2026. [Link](https://decrypt.co/347792/japans-largest-banks-get-greenlight-for-stablecoin-trial-plan-march-2026-launch).

\- Robinhood's Q3 profit beat Wall Street expectations but crypto revenue missed estimates despite jumping 300% to $268 million. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-11-05/robinhood-crypto-revenues-jump-but-still-miss-analyst-estimates).

\- Crypto exchange Gemini applied for regulatory approval to launch a derivatives exchange for prediction contracts, putting it in direct competition with Kalshi and Polymarket. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-11-04/winklevoss-founded-gemini-moves-to-enter-prediction-markets).

\- Canada unveiled plans to introduce stablecoin legislation requiring issuers to hold adequate reserves, establish redemption policies, and protect personal information. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377743/canada-stablecoin-legislation-budget).

\- Ripple raised $500 million at a $40 billion valuation from Fortress Investment Group and Citadel Securities. [Link](https://fortune.com/crypto/2025/11/05/ripple-xrp-fortress-citadel-securities-500-million-pantera-galaxy-brevan-howard-marshall-wace/).

\- Ferrari is releasing a crypto token to let its top 100 elite clients participate in the auction for the Ferrari 499P, which won three consecutive Le Mans titles. [Link](https://fortune.com/crypto/2025/11/03/ferrari-crypto-token/).
