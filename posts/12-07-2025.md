**THIS WEEK IN CRYPTO**
\- Bitcoin surged above $93,000 as short liquidations and renewed institutional interest helped fuel a rebound ahead of the upcoming Federal Reserve meeting. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/381197/bitcoin-jumps-back-above-93k-short-liquidations-fresh-institutional-interest-help-fuel-rebound-ahead-of-fed-meeting).

\- The CFTC announced that spot crypto products will be allowed to trade on registered futures exchanges. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-12-04/cftc-paves-way-for-spot-crypto-trading-on-registered-exchanges). [Link](https://unchainedcrypto.com/cftc-approves-spot-crypto-trading-on-u-s-exchanges/).

\- Citadel asked the SEC to regulate DeFi protocols as exchanges, sparking backlash from the cryptocurrency community. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/381306/citadel-sec-defi-backlash).

\- Bitcoin miners are facing the worst profitability crunch on record, with daily expected revenue dropping below median all-in costs and payback periods. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/381049/bitcoin-miners-profitability-crunch).

\- The US DoJ is seeking a 12-year prison sentence for Terra founder Do Kwon for the $40 billion fraud that led to the collapse of the TerraUSD stablecoin. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-12-05/do-kwon-should-get-12-years-for-colossal-crypto-fraud-us-says). [Link](https://decrypt.co/351106/trump-doj-wants-terra-founder-do-kwon-behind-bars-12-years-sbf).

\- Three key issues are holding up the progress of the crypto market structure bill in the U.S. Senate, including disputes over stablecoin yield, conflicts of interest, and DeFi protections, making a committee markup unlikely before the end of the year. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/381512/lawyer-outlines-three-issues-still-holding-up-crypto-market-structure-bill).

\- Anthropic published a report demonstrating its coding AI agents are capable of exploiting smart contracts. \- Connecticut issued cease-and-desist orders against prediction market firms like Kalshi, Robinhood, and Crypto.com for failing to comply with state gambling laws. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-12-04/connecticut-joins-fight-against-prediction-market-upstarts).

\- Franklin Templeton launched a Solana ETF (SOEZ) on the NYSE. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/381264/franklin-templeton-solana-etf).

\- The first Chainlink spot ETF, GLINK, will begin trading on the NYSE. [Link](https://unchainedcrypto.com/first-chainlink-etf-to-begin-trading-on-tuesday/).

Yi He is the partner of former Binance CEO "CZ", who was recently pardoned by President Donald Trump for failing to implement adequate AML / KYC on the exchange.
