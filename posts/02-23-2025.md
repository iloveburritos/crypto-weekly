**THIS WEEK IN CRYPTO**
\- President Javier Milei's endorsement of $LIBRA memecoin resulted in dramatic volatility, with the token gaining and losing billions. Fraud investigations ensued, and co-creator Hayden Davis faced allegations of claiming he could influence Milei through payments to his sister, which he later denied. [Link](https://fortune.com/crypto/2025/02/18/javier-milei-memecoin-libra-cryptocurrency-crash-argentina-federal-judge-investigation/). [Link](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2025/02-18/libra-token-s-co-creator-bragged-of-sending-money-to-argentine-president-milei-s-sister).

Despite this, Bybit processed over $4 billion in withdrawal requests following the hack. [Link](https://fortune.com/crypto/2025/02/21/bybit-largest-hack-crypto-history-1-4-billion-losses/).
