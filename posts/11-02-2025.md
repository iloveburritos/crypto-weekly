**THIS WEEK IN CRYPTO**
\- Bitcoin fell below $107,000 as Powell's cautious remarks against assuming further rate cuts after the Fed’s most recent 25 basis point cut led to a broader sell-off in risk assets. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-30/bitcoin-btc-extends-losses-as-powell-cautions-against-rate-cut-bets). [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-30/bitcoin-btc-extends-losses-as-powell-cautions-against-rate-cut-bets).

\- Bitcoin had its first October monthly loss since 2018, breaking a seven-year streak of gains. [Link](https://www.reuters.com/business/finance/bitcoin-breaks-october-streak-with-first-monthly-loss-since-2018-2025-10-31/).

\- Western Union plans to launch a USD-pegged stablecoin called USDPT on the Solana blockchain in early 2026. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/376608/western-union-plans-stablecoin-launch-on-solana-for-early-2026).

\- The Bitwise Solana Staking ETF, the first spot Solana ETF, launched despite the ongoing government shutdown. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-28/new-crypto-etfs-launch-in-crowded-field-despite-sec-shutdown).

\- Coinbase shares increased by 8% following third-quarter results that exceeded Wall Street estimates, with revenue growing 55% to $1.9 billion and net income rising nearly 5x to $433 million. Q3 transaction revenue grew 37% to $1 billion, with Ethereum trading share increasing to 22% of total volume from 15% in Q2. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-30/coinbase-rises-after-revenue-climbs-more-than-estimates). [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377030/coinbase-q3-revenue-surges-37-as-ethereum-trading-share-climbs-reversing-last-quarters-slump). 

\- Coinbase CEO Brian Armstrong ended the company’s quarterly earnings call by listing words that users had wagered on through prediction market platforms Kalshi and Polymarket. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-31/coinbase-ceo-stunt-exposes-vulnerability-in-prediction-markets). [Link](https://decrypt.co/347026/coinbase-ceo-garners-praise-pushback-prediction-market-shoutouts). 

\- Representative Ro Khanna plans to introduce legislation banning elected officials from owning or creating cryptocurrencies. [Link](https://decrypt.co/346294/democrat-seeks-crypto-trading-ban-politicians-binance-pardon).

\- Mastercard is in talks to acquire crypto infrastructure startup Zerohash for nearly $2 billion. [Link](https://fortune.com/crypto/2025/10/29/mastercard-zerohash-acquisition-bvnk-stablecoins-coinbase/).

\- Decentralized betting marketplace Polymarket plans to reopen in the US by late November after resolving regulatory issues. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/376623/polymarket-us-november-sports-betting-battleground-prediction-markets).

\- MegaETH's public token sale was over 27 times oversubscribed, valuing the Layer 2 blockchain at over $27 billion.  [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/376929/megaeths-public-token-sale-oversubscribed-by-27x-auction-officially-closes).

\- Tether, the world's largest stablecoin issuer, reported over $10 billion in profits for the first three quarters of 2025, with Q3 profits exceeding $4.3 billion. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377120/tethers-annual-profits-top-10-billion-as-treasury-holdings-swell).

\- Stablecoin issuers are capturing 60-75% of total daily protocol revenue by keeping the interest on dollar deposits. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/376316/stablecoin-issuers-dominate-crypto-revenue-capturing-75-daily-protocol-earnings).

\- Ethereum's next major mainnet upgrade Fusaka is scheduled for December 3, and will implement about a dozen Ethereum Improvement Proposals including the much awaited PeerDAS and increased block gas limits.  [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377065/ethereum-devs-officially-target-dec-3-for-fusaka-upgrade).

\- Standard Chartered predicts Ethereum will dominate a projected $2 trillion tokenized asset market by 2028, capturing the "vast majority" of growth due to its reliability and network effects. [Link](https://unchainedcrypto.com/ethereum-set-to-dominate-2-trillion-tokenized-asset-market-standard-chartered/).

\- The European Central Bank plans to launch a pilot phase for a digital euro in 2027, pending legislative approval in 2026. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-30/digital-euro-pilot-phase-could-start-as-soon-as-2027-ecb-says).

\- EU lawmaker Fernando Navarrete proposes that a fully-fledged digital euro should only be launched if the private sector fails to integrate Europe's fragmented payments landscape. [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-30/digital-euro-could-be-curtailed-under-eu-lawmaker-s-proposal).

\- Flutterwave and Polygon Labs are partnering to launch a stablecoin-powered cross-border payment network across 34 African countries, aiming to reduce remittance costs and increase cross-border payment volumes. [Link](https://unchainedcrypto.com/flutterwave-and-polygon-to-launch-africa-wide-stablecoin-payments/).

\- JPMorgan Chase is tokenizing a private-equity fund on its Kinexys Fund Flow blockchain platform. [Link](https://www.wsj.com/finance/investing/jpmorgan-tokenizes-private-equity-fund-on-its-own-blockchain-729dadda).

\- Core Scientific shareholders rejected a proposed $9 billion merger with AI computing company CoreWeave, a deal which would have helped Core Scientific diversify beyond Bitcoin mining. [Link](https://decrypt.co/346844/bitcoin-miner-core-scientific-nix-9-billion-coreweave-merger).

\- Citigroup and Coinbase are partnering to make fiat on-and-off ramps for stablecoins easier for Citi customers.  [Link](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-27/citi-taps-coinbase-to-explore-stablecoin-payments-for-clients-coin).

\- Decentralized derivatives exchange dYdX plans to offer spot crypto trading in the US by the end of 2025. [Link](https://www.theblock.co/post/377073/dydx-enter-us).

\- BlackRock's Bitcoin ETF experienced its largest single-day outflow since August, shedding $291 million amid broader Bitcoin ETF withdrawals. [Link](https://decrypt.co/346963/blackrocks-ibit-sheds-291m-in-a-day-as-bitcoin-etfs-pull-back).
